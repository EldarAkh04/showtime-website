SEMPY is an AI-Research Agent that supprts customers of the idealo website to help them make better buying decission. This is tool takes different information from different websites and summarizes them and gives the best advice.

Our Goal

The goal of this project is to offer an AI driven research agent on idealo’s price comparison platform that supports users who have not yet made a decision on which product to buy and are looking for additional information.
